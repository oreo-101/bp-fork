Web3 FinTech Solution

BailPort is a Web3 FinTech solution combining the power of frictionless, blockchain-based pathway for cross-border payments for consumers and merchants. We have built APIs to power collections, payouts, and currency exchanges via blockchain for individuals, small and medium enterprises (SMEs) and other FinTechs.

Solution for Individuals.

  • Blockchain Encryption

  • Simple, Fast, Secure KYC Verification

  • Blockchain/ Fiat Payments and Transfers

  • Currency Exchange

  • Scan to pay your Merchants

Solution for Merchants.

  • Blockchain Encryption

  • API & SDK Integration

  • Fiat Collections and Payouts

  • Fiat Exchange

  • Easy scan-to-pay checkout for customers

For Individuals

Safe & Secure

User data is encrypted using a mutating encryption algorithm and never shared. Wallet payments are powered by Algorand. ALL transactions are final.

Simple & Secure KYC

Backed by Filecoin, we provide a secure easy-to-use UI to perform KYC ID and Biometrics verification. The process takes minutes...Scan, Smile, Send

Low Cost P2P Payments

As low as 0.5% in fees, you can complete payments to local mobile wallets and banks. BailPort to BailPort wallet transfers are FREE!

Merchant Payments

With just a scan of QR code, you can use any currency to make secure payments to your merchants.

Direct Deposit

You can send money to your personal checking account or families', friends', business partners' bank account across borders.

For Merchants

Safe & Secure

Data is encrypted using a mutating encryption algorithm and never shared. Payments are powered by Algorand. ALL transactions are final.

Simple Integration

We provide a modern, trusted blockchain powered API as a solution to collections, payouts, and exchanges via blockchain for SMEs and other FinTechs.


Merchant Dashboard

Generate reports, download payment history, Initiate payment, share payment links to your customers from our Dashboard.

Multi-currency Collection

With our blockchain powered API, you can collect funds in any currency. Don't lose value for what you sweat for.
